Made in USA

Acknowledged as the “Cleanest Denim Factory in the World,” we have broadened our horizons and enhanced our capacities internationally to manufacture locally in the United States. L.A. used to be a global centre for denim manufacturing, and we're excited to revive it in a fresh and innovative way.

Creating Job Opportunities

We are honoured to generate job opportunities in the United States, even in opposition to the majority of the current industry. We think that diversity needs to be more intersectional, and that hiring should take into account factors other than gender. We hope that by introducing SF DENIM USA, other manufacturers will be encouraged to recognise the potential of this model as well.

Cutting

Lead times are shortened through quality control and real-time data, providing production flexibility. As part of SF DENIM's dedication to environmental protection, all fumes are carefully extracted during the cutting process.

Semi-automatic Sewing

Compared to traditional sewing, semi-automatic sewing is much faster, uses less energy, and makes it simple to quickly change shapes and styles. This adds the finishing touch and branding to every pair of jeans before they are shipped to customers.

3D Laser Detailing

Sandpaper and dangerous chemicals are typically used to create the distressed look on jeans, which can take 20 to 30 minutes per pair. We employ 3D laser detailing, which takes only 90 seconds to create the ideal look every time.

3D Mactec

Ozone

The creation of whisker and bunching patterns on jeans is made easier by the use of 3D pre-curing and curing ovens.It takes less time and energy to dry jeans when they are dried from the inside out; an hour's worth of work can produce 400 pairs of jeans on average in 5 to 10 minutes.

Robotic Spraying

Proprietary software-controlled robotic arms meticulously and automatically spray each pair of jeans without making any mistakes. The process of making denim sprays is less polluting than traditional methods because the factory does not use permanganate.

Reverse osmosis filtration

The reverse osmosis filtration system means the SF DENIM USA factory is able to recycle 98% of the water it uses, saving over 38 million liters of water a year.
