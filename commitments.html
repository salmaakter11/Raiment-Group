REKUT

A Unique Production Line

To establish an open and equal society where individuals from underprivileged backgrounds are given equal opportunities. The Rekut production line provides a means of training and assistance to individuals with disabilities in order to help them find long-term employment with steady pay.

REKUT

Providing Opportunities

To achieve an inclusive workplace, and provide sustainable job opportunities to the differently abled. Each associate gets five months of dedicated training to be fully independent. It costs $3,000 only to mainstreaming each of them.
